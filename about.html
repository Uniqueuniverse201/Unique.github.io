Hello

My name is Unique Pual Rennard Hutchinson and this is my About me page, where you can learn more about my history before I got to F.I.R.S.T Institute (The image above is my generic license, it was properly created with the help of my teacher named Mr. Miller).

I was born in Detroit, Michigan April 16th, 2004 and I always was a man who loved to express himself through art, even outside of actual art I loved to express myself in many other ways like Reading, Gaming, Photography and so much more, but my fear of expressing myself always boiled inside of me because of the constant bullying made me feel ashamed of being myself, especially from people I cared for. I will always appreciate the people that had my back while I was being myself and defended me during dark times like my Mom and Sister. Coming to F.I.R.S.T is helping me with expressing my ideas and I hope I can get to show this skill to the world.
